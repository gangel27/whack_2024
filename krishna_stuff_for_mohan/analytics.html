Data Analytics

There are a lot of statistics in the data. From popular ones like xG to niche ones like shots_conceded_within_8_seconds_of_corner. Which statistics are actually relevant, and which are just noise? Here we did some complex data analytics to find which statistics are most relevant and important when it comes to the one thing that actually matters: results. Knowing which statistics are most important when it comes to deciding the win helps knowing what to focus and improve on.
